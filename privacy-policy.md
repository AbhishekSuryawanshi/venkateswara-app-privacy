Privacy Policy

Sri Venkateswara – Tirumala Devotional App

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sri Venkateswara – Tirumala Devotional App collects, uses, and protects information when you use the application.

⸻

Information We Collect

The app does not require user registration and does not collect personally identifiable information.

However, some basic data may be processed to provide app functionality.

Usage Data

The app may process anonymous usage data such as:
	•	crowd status updates submitted by users
	•	devotional chant counts
	•	general app usage statistics

This data is not linked to any personal identity.

⸻

Firebase Services

The app uses Google Firebase Firestore to store and synchronize certain app features such as:
	•	Tirumala crowd status updates
	•	devotional chant counters
	•	configuration data

Firebase may collect limited technical information such as device type or IP address as part of its infrastructure services.

You can review Firebase privacy practices here:

https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

⸻

Notifications

The app may send local notifications for features such as:
	•	seva reminders
	•	devotional reminders

These notifications are generated on your device and do not require personal information.

⸻

External Content

The app may display content from external services such as:
	•	YouTube videos
	•	official temple websites
	•	maps for navigation

These services may have their own privacy policies.

⸻

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information used within the app.
No sensitive personal information is stored or collected by the app.

⸻

Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

⸻

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
