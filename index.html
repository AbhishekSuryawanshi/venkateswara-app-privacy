Privacy Policy

Sri Venkateswara – Tirumala Devotional App

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sri Venkateswara – Tirumala Devotional App handles information when you use the application.

Information We Collect

The app does not require user registration and does not collect personally identifiable information.

However, limited anonymous data may be processed to support certain app features.

Usage Data

The app may process anonymous usage data such as:

  • Crowd status updates submitted by users
  • Devotional chant counts
  • General app usage statistics

This information is not linked to any personal identity.

Firebase Services

The app uses Google Firebase Firestore to store and synchronize certain app features such as:

  • Tirumala crowd status updates
  • Devotional chant counters
  • Application configuration data

The app does not collect or store personal information in Firebase.

You can review Firebase privacy practices here:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Location Information

The app may temporarily access the device’s precise location when a user submits a Tirumala crowd update.

Location is used only to verify that the user is within the Tirumala temple area before allowing the submission of a crowd update.

Location data is not stored, transmitted, or associated with any personal identity.

Notifications

The app may generate local notifications for features such as:

  • Seva reminders
  • Devotional reminders

These notifications are generated locally on the device and do not require personal information.

External Content

The app may display content from external services such as:

  • YouTube videos
  • Official temple websites
  • Map services for navigation

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information processed within the app. No sensitive personal information is stored or collected by the application.

Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
